Tab Favicon Labeler

Create custom favicon labels for your tabs based on URL patterns

How it works

STG
Label: STAGE
Pattern: https?://stage\.application\.com/.*
PRD
Label: PROD
Pattern: https?://prod\.application\.com/.*

Labels support up to 6 characters, displayed as 3 per line. Perfect for environment indicators!
